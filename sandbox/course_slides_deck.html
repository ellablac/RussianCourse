Community Russian Course

👋 Добро пожаловать!

An Open Community

  • All Levels Welcome: No prior experience needed.
  • Driven by Community: Less like a formal classroom, more like a language club.
  • Free & Flexible: Jump in anytime at zero cost.

Why Learn With Us?

  • Make New Friends: Connect with fellow learners and native speakers.
  • Sharpen Communication: Enhance your skills by actively participating and collaborating.
  • Boost Brain Power: Language learning provides excellent mental flexibility and memory exercise.
  • Cultural Insight: Gain a deeper understanding of Russian culture, media, and traditions.

How We Learn Together

  • Hybrid Format: A flexible mix of online and in-person sessions.
  • Peer & Native Interaction: Learn in small groups and practice with native conversational partners.
  • Modern Tech: We utilize our course website, apps, and free AI tools for instant feedback.
  • Pop Culture Immersion: We make grammar fun by breaking down scenes from hit movies, music videos, and engaging activities.

Learn by Teaching

  • The Philosophy: Second-language learners uniquely understand the hurdles beginners face.
  • The Practice: You are invited (never required!) to teach concepts you’ve mastered to your peers.
  • The Result: We learn something new, pass it along, and build a truly collaborative learning loop.

Course Structure: The Foundations

Part 1: Starting Strong

  • Mastering the Cyrillic Alphabet
  • Basic Phonetics & Pronunciation
  • Essential Greetings & Introductions

Part 2: The Building Blocks

  • Understanding Noun Genders
  • Forming Plurals
  • Constructing Basic Sentences
  • A Gentle Introduction to Cases

Course Structure: Expanding Horizons

Part 3: Action & Description

  • Verb Conjugations (Past, Present, Future)
  • Describing the world with Adjectives
  • Prepositional & Accusative Cases

Part 4: Fluidity & Motion

  • Introduction to Verbs of Motion
  • Genitive, Dative, and Instrumental Cases
  • Vocabulary builds gradually across all parts!
