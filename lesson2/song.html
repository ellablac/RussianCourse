  5. Using your notes, translate the lyrics into English. Then check your understanding by clicking the translation button.
  6. Notice how the word-by-word translation would sound very awkward in English because Russian sentence structure is different.
  7. Practice your pronunciation by reading the Russian lyrics aloud and checking against the video.
