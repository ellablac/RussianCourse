Unenrolling from a Class in Google Classroom

Follow these quick steps to leave a class from your Google Classroom dashboard.

Steps

  1. 1
    Go to classroom.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
  2. 2
    On the Classes page, find the class you want to leave.
  3. 3
    On the class card, click the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  4. 4
    Click Unenroll.
  5. 5
    A confirmation window will appear. Click Unenroll again.

Need it again later?

If you change your mind later, you can always sign back up by clicking this link: Join the class again
